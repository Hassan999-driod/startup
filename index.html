History of Saudi National Day

Saudi National Day, September 23rd, celebrates the 1932 unification of Saudi Arabia by King Abdulaziz Al Saud. It ended regional conflicts, creating modern Saudi Arabia. Initially less celebrated, it became a national holiday in 2005. Today, it's a grand display of national pride, honoring the nation's history and progress.
